Important Note

This converter uses a completely browser-based approach for PDF to PowerPoint conversion:

  • Your files never leave your browser - all processing happens locally
  • Preserves images and page layouts from your PDF
  • Works even when the server is unavailable
  • Large PDFs may take longer to process

The conversion uses PDF.js to render PDF pages as images and PptxGenJS to create a PowerPoint file with those images.

How It Works

  1. Upload your PDF file
  2. Your browser renders each page of the PDF
  3. Each page is converted to an image and placed on a PowerPoint slide
  4. Text is extracted and added as slide notes where possible
  5. Download your PowerPoint presentation directly from your browser

This tool uses 100% client-side processing - your PDF never leaves your computer.
